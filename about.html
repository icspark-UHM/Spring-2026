About the Class

Web Development Journey

This 14-week intensive course is designed to take you from the basics of web development to building modern, interactive web applications. Whether you're a complete beginner or have some coding experience, our structured curriculum and hands-on approach will help you develop the skills needed in today's tech industry.

You'll learn:

  • HTML5 and modern semantic markup
  • CSS3, including Flexbox and Grid layouts
  • JavaScript fundamentals and DOM manipulation
  • Working with APIs and asynchronous programming
  • React basics and state management
  • Backend concepts with Node.js
  • Database fundamentals
  • Deployment and basic DevOps

Meet Our Team

Instructors

Our instructors are experienced developers and educators passionate about teaching and mentoring the next generation of web developers. They bring real-world experience and practical insights to every lesson.

Each instructor specializes in different aspects of web development, ensuring you get expert guidance throughout the course.

Teaching Assistants

Our TAs are here to support your learning journey. They provide one-on-one help during lab sessions, review your code, and offer additional explanations when needed.

Many of our TAs are former students who excelled in the course and now help others succeed.

Student Success Team

The success team ensures you have all the resources you need to excel in the course. They coordinate study groups, maintain the learning platform, and organize special events.
